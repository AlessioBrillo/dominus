## Maintainer Expectations

- Review PRs within 7 business days
- Respond to security reports within 7 days
- Cut releases when significant changes accumulate
- Keep ADRs up to date with architectural changes
- Enforce the architecture principles documented in the ADR series

## Becoming a Maintainer

DOMINUS is currently a single-developer project. As the community grows,
additional maintainers may be added at the project lead's discretion.
Consistent, high-quality contributions are the best path to a maintainer role.
